# Task 05 – Echo Ranging

## Problem Statement

A person shouts toward a cliff and hears the echo $1 \,\text{s}$ later. The speed of sound is $343 \,\text{m/s}$. Find the distance to the cliff.

## Theory

The echo time includes the forward and return trip, so the total path length is

$$
d_{\text{total}} = vt
$$

The one-way distance to the cliff is half of this value:

$$
d = \frac{vt}{2}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

Substitute the data:

$$
d = \frac{343 \times 1}{2}
$$

$$
d = 171.5 \,\text{m}
$$

## Final Result

$$
d = 171.5 \,\text{m}
$$

## Interpretation

The sound travels to the cliff and back in $1 \,\text{s}$. Therefore, the cliff is half the total traveled distance away.
