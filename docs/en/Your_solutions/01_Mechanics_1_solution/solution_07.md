# Task 07 – Parametric Motion

## Problem Statement

$$
x = 2t^2, \quad y = 3t^3
$$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

Eliminate $t$:

$$
t = \sqrt{\frac{x}{2}}
$$

$$
y = 3 \left(\frac{x}{2}\right)^{3/2}
$$

---

Velocity:

$$
\vec{v} = (4t, 9t^2)
$$

Speed:

$$
|\vec{v}| = \sqrt{16t^2 + 81t^4}
$$

Acceleration:

$$
\vec{a} = (4, 18t)
$$

---

## Final Result

Acceleration is not constant.

---

## Interpretation

Motion is non-uniform in both magnitude and direction.
