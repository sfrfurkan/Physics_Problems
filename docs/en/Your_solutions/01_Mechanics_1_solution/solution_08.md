# Task 08 – Circular Motion

## Problem Statement

Find centripetal acceleration at Earth's equator.

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
\omega = \frac{2\pi}{86400}
$$

$$
a = \omega^2 R
$$

$$
R = 6.378 \times 10^6
$$

$$
a \approx 0.034 \text{ m/s}^2
$$

---

## Final Result

$$
a \approx 0.034 \text{ m/s}^2
$$

---

## Interpretation

Centripetal acceleration is very small compared to gravity.
