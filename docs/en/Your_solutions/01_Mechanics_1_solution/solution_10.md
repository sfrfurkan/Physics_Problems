# Task 10 – 3D Motion

## Problem Statement

$$
\vec{r}(t) = (a\cos(\omega t), b\sin(\omega t), bt)
$$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

Trajectory:

$$
\frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1
$$

Helical motion.

---

Velocity:

$$
\vec{v} = (-a\omega \sin(\omega t), b\omega \cos(\omega t), b)
$$

---

## Final Result

Motion is a helix.

---

## Interpretation

Combination of circular motion and linear drift.
