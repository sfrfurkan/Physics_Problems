# Task 04 – Vector Calculus

## Problem Statement

$$
\vec{r}(t) = (3t^2)\hat{i} + (5t - 8t^2)\hat{j}
$$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

Velocity:

$$
\vec{v}(t) = \frac{d\vec{r}}{dt} = (6t)\hat{i} + (5 - 16t)\hat{j}
$$

Acceleration:

$$
\vec{a}(t) = (6)\hat{i} + (-16)\hat{j}
$$

---

## Final Result

$$
\vec{v}(t) = (6t, 5 - 16t)
$$

$$
\vec{a}(t) = (6, -16)
$$

---

## Interpretation

Acceleration is constant → uniformly accelerated motion.
