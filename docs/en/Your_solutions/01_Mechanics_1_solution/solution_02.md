# Task 02 – Range Optimization

## Problem Statement

Show that maximum range occurs at $\theta = 45^\circ$.

---

## Theory

Range formula:

$$
R(\theta) = \frac{v_0^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g}
$$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

Differentiate:

$$
\frac{dR}{d\theta} = \frac{v_0^2}{g} \cdot 2 \cos(2\theta)
$$

Set derivative to zero:

$$
\cos(2\theta) = 0
$$

$$
2\theta = 90^\circ \Rightarrow \theta = 45^\circ
$$

---

## Final Result

$$
\theta_{\text{max}} = 45^\circ
$$

---

## Interpretation

Maximum range occurs when horizontal and vertical contributions are balanced.
