# Task 05 – Relative Velocity

## Problem Statement

River: $2$ m/s east  
Boat: $5$ m/s  

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

Cancel river drift:

$$
v_x = -2
$$

$$
\sin \theta = \frac{2}{5}
$$

$$
\theta \approx 23.6^\circ
$$

North component:

$$
v_y = \sqrt{25 - 4} = \sqrt{21}
$$

Time:

$$
t = \frac{200}{\sqrt{21}} \approx 43.6 \text{ s}
$$

---

## Final Result

- Angle: $23.6^\circ$ west of north  
- Time: $43.6 \text{ s}$

---

## Interpretation

Boat must aim upstream to compensate for current.
